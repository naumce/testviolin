Gorjana Stojanovska — Pianist & Music Educator

Gorjana Stojanovska is a pianist of remarkable artistry and a dedicated music educator. She began piano studies at the age of eight and later completed her higher education at the Faculty of Musical Arts in Skopje, where she studied under Prof. Boris Romanov and Prof. Marija Gjoshevska, graduating in 2017. She earned her Master’s degree in 2019, further deepening her technical and artistic mastery.

In pursuit of further excellence, Gorjana continued her studies at the Talent Music Master Academy in Brescia, Italy, working with Prof. Ruben Dalibaltayan and earning an Artist Diploma in 2020. She has enriched her musical development through masterclasses with renowned pianists including Boris Romanov, Yuri Kot, Andrej Diev, Ruben Dalibaltayan, and Yulia Gubaydulina.

Gorjana’s career as a soloist has brought her to prestigious festivals and concert stages, where she is known for performances that combine technical brilliance with expressive depth. She has appeared with leading orchestras, and in 2024 will perform as soloist in Vienna with the Chamber Orchestra of the National Opera and Ballet.

Alongside her performing career, Gorjana is passionate about teaching. Her approach is thoughtful, supportive, and inspiring — focused on building both technical excellence and musical understanding. She guides students in developing their individuality, encouraging them to explore musical style, historical context, and expressive possibilities with confidence and curiosity.

Gorjana Stojanovska’s work as a performer and educator reflects her deep commitment to artistic integrity and to sharing the transformative power of music.
